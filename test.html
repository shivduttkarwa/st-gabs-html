Our History

A quiet legacy.

A simple journey through the milestones, people and places that shaped St Gabriel’s School across more than a century.

1920

Southleigh is purchased

The Christian Brothers found a suitable property in Sydney on which to establish the school.

1922

The school opens

St Gabriel’s School opened its doors to its first students on 22 September 1922.

1973

Day school transition

The school transitioned to a day school and welcomed girls into the learning community.

1976

Integration program

Students attended St Gabriel’s in the morning and their local mainstream school in the afternoon.

2010

Broader admission

Admission criteria widened to include students with a range of special education needs.

2024

K–12 future

The first Year 12 class marks a major milestone in the school’s continued growth.
