What are the benefits
of working with us?

Financial Benefits

  • Competitive salaries

  • NFP Renumeration Packaging (through Southgate) which increases take home pay

Access to the School

  • Onsite parking

  • Close to Metro

  • Good Public transport

Staffroom Supplies

  • Tea and coffee supplies

  • Wellbeing weeks are enjoyed regularly throughout the year to celebrate and support staff wellbeing

  • School Canteen available Wednesday, Thursday and Friday for staff to purchase lunch

Lifelong Learning Opportunities

  • Attendance at Professional Development Activities

  • Formal Professional Development Programs

  • Participation in Professional Learning Communities

Technology

All Staff are supplied with:

  • Laptop

  • Ipad

  • All teaching and learning spaces have a touch panel board

Health and Wellbeing

  • Access to School wellbeing Programs and Initiatives

  • Free Flu Vaccination

  • Training in First Aid, CPR and other students management

  • Fitness programs

  • Access to EAP – Employee Assistance Programme to support your wellbeing and to help you deal with the challenges, concerns or issues that may be affecting you at work or at home. EAP is available to staff and their families
