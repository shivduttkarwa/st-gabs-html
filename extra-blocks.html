Second Line

St Gabriel's is committed to transparency and keeping our community informed about the values, programs, and decisions that shape our school.

At St Gabriel's, inclusive education means every student is welcomed, valued, and supported to achieve their personal best. Our specialist staff work with each family to develop individual learning plans that meet the unique needs of every child, ensuring they can participate fully in school life.

Our multidisciplinary team includes speech pathologists, occupational therapists, psychologists, and specialist teachers who collaborate daily to support students with complex needs. Support is always tailored, evidence-based, and delivered in partnership with families.

Learn More

We provide speech therapy, occupational therapy, physiotherapy, and psychology services on site. These are integrated into the school day so students receive consistent, coordinated support without disrupting their learning time.

Families receive regular updates through our parent portal, termly progress reports, and scheduled meetings with the student's key teacher. Our staff are also available via phone and email for any questions or concerns that arise between meetings.

Enrolment begins with an initial enquiry and school tour, followed by an assessment to understand your child's needs. Our enrolment team will guide you through every step, from application to transition planning, ensuring a smooth start for your family.

This year, we are seeking support to fund the upgrade of one of our Primary playgrounds, including the installation of inclined, ground-level climbing and movement structures over soft-fall surfacing. The project will also create a tiered outdoor play space with ramp access, providing an accessible connection to our Kindergarten to Year 2 playground. This will ensure students with low mobility can safely and confidently access and enjoy our outdoor learning environments.
